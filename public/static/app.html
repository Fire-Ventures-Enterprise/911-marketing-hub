Data Usage Authorization

Before generating content, we need your permission to use your website data.

By clicking I Authorize, you grant Services Leads Marketing Hub permission to:

  • Access and analyze your company's public website content
  • Extract your services, contact information, brand identity, and tone of voice
  • Store this data to power landing pages, ads, SEO content, and marketing materials
  • Automatically refresh this data every 30 days to keep content current

This covers generation of: landing pages, Google Ads campaigns, SEO blog content, and related marketing materials for your company only.

You may revoke this authorization at any time from Settings → Data & Privacy. Revocation immediately disables content generation until re-authorized.

Data is stored securely in Cloudflare infrastructure and never shared with third parties outside your subscription agreement.
